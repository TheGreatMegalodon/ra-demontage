Our services
We always carry out projects with quality and professionalism according to all work standards and safety regulations.
Refinery
To ensure existing production needs are met and maintained, refinery operators communicate with plant managers and regulatory authorities and collaborate with other refinery colleagues, suppliers and stakeholders.
Chemical industry
The chemical industry spans several sub-sectors and includes everything from chemical engineers to construction and site control.
Constructions
We are responsible for numerous on-site tasks, such as debris removal, scaffolding, loading and unloading construction materials.
Heating and cooling
Our technicians work with furnaces as well as thermostats and air conditioning systems to ensure everything runs smoothly throughout a client's home or commercial building.
Ventilation
Our heating and ventilation engineers install and service heating and air conditioning in large buildings such as factories, schools and hospitals, as well as family homes.
Solar energy
Our solar technicians are construction professionals who install and maintain solar panels at various locations.
Repair and maintenance
Our maintenance and overhaul technicians provide mechanical device repairs. We use instruments for precise measurement and testing of electric meters, power tools, and other equipment for handling various materials.
Repair and installation
Performs tasks required for the production process. We help in the manufacture, maintenance and repair of components and products.
Designing and monitoring of completed works
In cooperation with architects, surveyors and workers, we design and organize work on construction sites so that the project is completed according to all work standards and safety regulations.
